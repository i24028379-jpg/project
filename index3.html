Character Info

  • Basics

    Kuromi is a Sanrio character introduced in 2005 as My Melody's rival. A black bunny with a pink skull hood, she is rebellious and playful, favors black-and-pink fashion, and—despite appearances—is warm-hearted.

  • Personality

    Kuromi is a prankster with an edgy vibe, yet she cares about her friends. She often keeps a diary—sometimes venting about My Melody—in her playful "Kuromi Notes".

  • Background

    Kuromi first appeared in the 2005 TV anime "Onegai My Melody". Once companions with My Melody, childhood squabbles turned into a comedic rivalry. Stories sometimes place her in Mary Land and the human world, leading to many humorous adventures.
